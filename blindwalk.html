BLINDWALK

Blindwalk is an interactive art installation that embodies a critical view on aspects of trust and accountability when dealing with technology.

The installation is inspired by classic trust games, and has built-in bugs that make the participants question who truly has the responsibility when our technology acts in unintended ways.

Context: Chalmers University of Technology; Master Programme in Interaction Design & Technology; Course: Designing User Experiences, Individual Project

My role: Idea, Research, Concept sketching
