POCO

Poco och sm√•folket (Poco and the fair folk) is an educational game about Swedish folk music and folklore, developed by Musikmedel. The game is funded by SPSM (Specialpedagogiska Skolmyndigheten/The Swedish Agency for Special Education) and is currently available for MacOS, Windows and IOS.

Context: Consulting job for Musikmedel Future Vision AB

My role: Concept, Story, Interaction Design

Challenge

I was hired by Musikmedel Future Vision to develop a strong proof of concept for an educational game for children in year 1-3 with special focus on education for children with neurodevelopmental disorders such as ADHD, autism and dyslexia. The basic concept was that it should be a game about Swedish folk music and folklore that should cover several aspects of the Swedish educational curriculum.

Story

The story was developed to fit a six-chapter narrative where each chapter should advance the narrative as well as provide some pedagogical points that could be practised after finishing the chapter.

Prototype

Gamified interface for repeating the educational aspects that were brought up in different chapters.

Students should experience a clear progression through the educational activities, know what they are currently working with and what is expected of them.

Final Version

After my delivery, Musikmedel continued the development of the project with funding from the Swedish Agency of Special Education. The project is now finished and the final version is available on MacOS, PC and IOS for Ipad.
